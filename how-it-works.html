The Process

How FoodRescue Works

A research-validated 8-step workflow derived from 31 stakeholder interviews, 10 field days, and direct experience with real food rescue operations in Surat and Ahmedabad.

Start Donating → Join as NGO
01
🍽️
Restaurant Posts Surplus Food
Staff opens FoodRescue app, uploads a photo of the surplus food, enters quantity, category, preparation time, and pickup address. Takes <3 minutes on any smartphone.
Field Data: "We have food left after every lunch and dinner service. We need something we can use in 2 minutes — not 20." — Restaurant owner, Surat
📷 Photo Upload ⚖️ Quantity & Category 📍 GPS Address ⏰ Prep Time
02
🛡️
FSSAI Food Safety Validation
Automatic scoring (0–100) based on preparation time, packaging status, image quality, donor FSSAI certification, and meal category. Food that fails basic safety standards (<30/100) is rejected automatically.
FSSAI Standards Applied: Hot cooked food: 4h max • Refrigerated: 24h max • Fresh produce: same day
🛡️ Safety Score ⏱️ Freshness Check 📦 Packaging Verify
03
🔔
NGOs Receive Instant Notification
Top 5 NGOs are ranked by a smart scoring algorithm: proximity (30%), available capacity (30%), urgency (20%), and reliability rating (20%). All 5 receive push notification within 30 seconds.
Match Score Formula: 0.3×(1/Distance) + 0.3×NeedLevel + 0.2×Capacity + 0.2×(1/TimeToExpiry)
04
✅
NGO Accepts & Sets Pickup Window
An NGO coordinator reviews the food details and safety score, then confirms a pickup time window. If no NGO responds within the urgency window, the platform widens the radius.
Field Finding: "We spend 2–3 hours every day calling donors or WhatsApp groups. FoodRescue makes it zero." — Asha Foundation, Ahmedabad
05
👤
Volunteer Assigned
The nearest available, verified volunteer is assigned within 2 minutes. If they reject, it moves to the next volunteer. Three consecutive rejections = temporary suspension.
📍 GPS Proximity ✅ ID Verified ⭐ Rating Filtered
06
🛵
Volunteer Picks Up — QR Scanned
Volunteer arrives at restaurant. Food safety re-check is done on the spot. Volunteer scans the restaurant's Pickup QR code. This triggers automatic GPS tracking (location updates every 30 seconds).
07
🚗
GPS-Tracked Delivery
Restaurant and NGO can both see volunteer's live location, real-time ETA via Google Maps, and status updates throughout delivery. Dedicated chat thread for any coordination.
08
❤️
Delivery Confirmed — Impact Generated
NGO coordinator scans Delivery QR. This closes the donation loop: restaurant gets CSR impact certificate, volunteer gets reward points, NGO gets receipt for records. Full audit trail stored on Firestore.
📜 CSR Certificate 🏆 Reward Points 📊 Impact Report 🌿 SDG Compliance
