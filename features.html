Automated Food Safety Scoring

Before a donation is visible to any NGO, the platform scores the food on a scale of 0 to 100. It checks prep time, packaging, donor FSSAI registration status, and photo validity. Any score below 50 triggers automatic rejection to ensure safety.

Proximity Match Engine
