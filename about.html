Our Story

Built from the Ground Up

FoodRescue wasn't designed in a boardroom. It was built from 10 days of field research, 31 face-to-face interviews, and one unforgettable night watching 120 kg of food get discarded.

The Origin Story

The Night That Started Everything

During a GTU Societal Internship field visit to Surat in 2024, our team witnessed a 5-star hotel discard over 120 kg of cooked food at 11 PM — not because they were careless, but because they had no legal, logistic, or operational system to give it away safely.

Two hours later, the same night, we visited an NGO running a shelter for 80 families. They had received no food donation that week. They were surviving on rationed grain.

The food existed. The need existed. What was missing was the real-time digital bridge between them. That's why we built FoodRescue.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯
Mission
To build India's first real-time, FSSAI-compliant food rescue platform that eliminates the friction between surplus food and community need — in under 5 minutes, every day.
🌍
Vision
A future where every kilogram of edible surplus food is rescued before it becomes waste — across every city, town, and village in India. Zero hunger. Zero waste.
❤️
Values
Dignity in giving. Safety in every bite. Transparency in every handoff. Recognition for every volunteer. We believe in food rescue done right — not just fast.
